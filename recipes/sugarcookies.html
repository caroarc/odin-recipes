Easy Sugar Cookies

Description

Satisfy your sweet tooth with this top-rated sugar cookie recipe. It comes together with just a few ingredients you likely already have on hand â€” and the sugar cookies turn out perfect every time.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Leavener (Baking soda or flour)
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

      Beat sugar, butter, egg, and vanilla in one bowl; mix flour and leaveners in another.
      Gradually blend the flour mixture into the butter mixture.
      Roll the dough into balls and bake until the edges are golden.
